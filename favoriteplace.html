I am a roller coaster enthusiast and enjoy going to theme parks whenever I can. My favorite place would be Motiongate, a theme park located in Dubai. It is the place where I find most comfort visiting. Motiongate is the closest theme park to where I live and it is the theme park that I visit most often.

Places I would like to visit

Tokyo DisneySea
- The theme park is located in Tokyo, Japan.
- The themeing in this park is unlike any other theme park.
- The park is very immersive with lots of areas to explore.
Fuji-Q-Highland
- The amusement park is located in Yamanashi, Japan
- The park has 5 record breaking and unique roller coasters.
- The park is located next to a volcano with beautiful scenery.
Six Flags Magic Mountain
- The amusement park is located in Los Angeles, California
- The park has the largest collection of roller coasters in the world.
Cedar Point
- Located in Sandusky, Ohio.
- Known as the "Roller coaster capital of the world"
