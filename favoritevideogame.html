My Favorite Video Games

I always find time to play video games, even in university. My main console on which I play video games is the Playstation 5. My favorite video game would have to be Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War. I really enjoy the first person shooter. I spend most time playing the zombies and multiplayer modes.

My Top 5 Video Games (in no particular order):

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War

A first person shooter with 3 game modes: Multiplayer, Zombies, Campaign.
